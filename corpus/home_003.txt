Doing Laundry — Basics

Sort clothes by colour (whites, lights, darks) and by fabric care (delicates separately). Read care labels before the first wash — some items are dry-clean only or hand-wash only. Use cold water for most loads — it saves energy and prevents shrinking and fading. Use about 2 tablespoons of detergent per load (less than the cap typically suggests). Don't overload the machine — clothes need room to tumble.
