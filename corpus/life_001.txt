How to Cook Pasta

Bring a large pot of water to a rolling boil. Add about 1 tablespoon of salt per litre of water. Add the pasta and stir to prevent sticking. Cook according to the package time, usually 8-12 minutes for dry pasta. Test by tasting — pasta should be al dente, firm to the bite but not crunchy. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining; the starch helps sauces cling. Drain in a colander but do not rinse unless using cold.
