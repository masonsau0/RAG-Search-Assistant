Tips for Better Sleep

Aim for 7-9 hours per night. Keep a consistent sleep schedule, even on weekends. Avoid caffeine after 2 PM and large meals within 3 hours of bedtime. Keep the bedroom cool (around 18-20°C / 65-68°F), dark, and quiet. Avoid screens for 30-60 minutes before bed, or use blue-light filters. If you can't fall asleep within 20 minutes, get up and do something quiet in dim light until you feel drowsy.
