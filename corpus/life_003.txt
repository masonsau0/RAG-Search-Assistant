Basic Knife Skills

Use a sharp chef's knife — a dull knife is more dangerous because it slips. Curl the fingertips of your guiding hand under so the blade rides against your knuckles, never your fingertips. For onions: cut in half through the root, peel, place flat side down, make horizontal and vertical cuts, then cut across. Wash and dry knives by hand — dishwashers dull and damage them.
