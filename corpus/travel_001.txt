How to Pack for a Trip

Roll clothes instead of folding to save space and reduce wrinkles. Pack heavier items (shoes, books) at the bottom near the wheels for stability. Carry on essentials: medications, a change of clothes, phone charger, and any irreplaceable items. Wear your bulkiest shoes and jacket on the plane. Check airline liquids rules — most limit carry-on liquids to containers under 100 ml, all in a clear 1-litre bag. Photograph your packed luggage before checking it.
