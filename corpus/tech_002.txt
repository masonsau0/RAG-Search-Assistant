Why Use a Password Manager

A password manager stores all your passwords encrypted, so you only need to remember one master password. Benefits: every site can have a unique strong password (so a breach at one site can't cascade), auto-fill prevents phishing on lookalike domains, and built-in generators create secure passwords automatically. Popular options: 1Password, Bitwarden (free tier), and Apple Keychain or Google Password Manager (free, built into the OS).
