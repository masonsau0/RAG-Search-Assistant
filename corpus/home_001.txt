How to Unclog a Drain

First try a plunger — fill the sink with enough water to cover the plunger cup, then push down sharply 5-6 times. If that fails, try a mixture of 1/2 cup baking soda followed by 1/2 cup white vinegar; let fizz for 15 minutes, then flush with boiling water. For stubborn clogs, a hand snake (drum auger) is more reliable than chemical drain cleaners, which can damage pipes and are hazardous to skin.
